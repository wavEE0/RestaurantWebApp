Little Known fact...
Oaxaca is an infamous state in the country of Mexico, rich of cultural history. Our restuarant, named after the very state, aims to present customers with the dining experience of their lifetime. From the entrees to the last bite of your dessert, we guarantee that you will be satisfied.

Best Mexican Food I've Ever Had!

With the first bite, I was transported back into time when I was in mexico. The ingrediants and flavour notes were undoubtably authentic. I would eat here everyday if I could!

Amazing Service!
